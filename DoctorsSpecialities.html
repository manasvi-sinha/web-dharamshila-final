Dharmshilla Narayana Superspeciality Hospital


  • Speciality

Dharamshila Narayana Superspeciality hospital offers world class medical services in various fields. The expert and competent group of doctors at the hospital offer comprehensive healthcare towards special fields such as Cardiac Sciences, Oncology, Bone Marrow Transplantation, Emergency Medicine and Critical Care, Neurology and Neurosurgery, Nephrology, Urology and Renal Transplantation, Orthopedics and Joint Replacement.

Oncology

The Oncology department offers comprehensive cancer care, including diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation. Equipped with advanced technology and staffed by experienced oncologists, the department specializes in chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical oncology.

Cardiology

The Cardiology department provides state-of-the-art care for heart conditions. Services include non-invasive and invasive cardiology, interventional procedures, and cardiac surgery, supported by a team of skilled cardiologists and cardiac surgeons.

Neurology

The Neurology department offers advanced diagnosis and treatment for neurological disorders. With a focus on stroke management, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative diseases, the department utilizes the latest neuroimaging and treatment techniques.

Neurosurgery

The Neurosurgery department specializes in surgical interventions for brain and spinal disorders. Equipped with cutting-edge technology, the team performs complex procedures, including tumor removals and minimally invasive spine surgeries.

Orthopedics

The Orthopedics department provides comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions. Services include joint replacement, trauma care, sports medicine, and pediatric orthopedics, delivered by experienced orthopedic surgeons and therapists.

Gastroenterology

The Gastroenterology department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of digestive system disorders. Advanced endoscopic procedures, liver disease management, and nutritional support are key services offered by skilled gastroenterologists.

Nephrology

The Nephrology department offers specialized care for kidney-related diseases. Services include dialysis, kidney transplant, and management of chronic kidney conditions, with a team of expert nephrologists and support staff.

Urology

The Urology department provides comprehensive care for urinary tract and male reproductive system disorders. Treatments include endoscopic surgeries, stone management, and prostate care, delivered by experienced urologists.

Pulmonology

The Pulmonology department specializes in the treatment of respiratory system disorders. Services include management of asthma, COPD, interventional pulmonology, and sleep disorders, with a focus on patient-centric care.

Dermatology

The Dermatology department offers advanced care for skin, hair, and nail conditions. Treatments include cosmetic dermatology, laser therapies, and management of chronic skin diseases, provided by experienced dermatologists.

Pediatrics

The Pediatrics department provides comprehensive healthcare for children from birth to adolescence. Services include immunizations, growth and development monitoring, and treatment of pediatric illnesses, with a focus on compassionate care.

Obstetrics and Gynecology

The Obstetrics and Gynecology department offers specialized care for women's health, including prenatal, antenatal, and postnatal care. The team provides services such as minimally invasive gynecological surgeries and fertility treatments.

ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)

The ENT department provides comprehensive care for ear, nose, and throat conditions. Services include hearing assessments, sinus surgeries, and treatment of voice disorders, performed by experienced ENT specialists.

Ophthalmology

The Ophthalmology department offers advanced eye care services, including cataract surgeries, LASIK, and treatment of retinal disorders. The team uses the latest technology to ensure optimal outcomes for patients.

Rheumatology

The Rheumatology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of rheumatic diseases and autoimmune conditions. Services include management of arthritis, lupus, and osteoporosis, provided by expert rheumatologists.

Endocrinology

The Endocrinology department offers comprehensive care for hormonal and metabolic disorders. Treatments include diabetes management, thyroid disorders, and osteoporosis, delivered by a team of experienced endocrinologists.

Psychiatry

The Psychiatry department provides mental health services, including diagnosis and treatment of psychiatric conditions. Services include therapy, counseling, and medication management, with a focus on holistic patient care.

Onco-Surgery

The Onco-Surgery department specializes in surgical treatments for cancer patients. The team performs complex cancer surgeries with precision and care, ensuring comprehensive oncological management and patient support.

Radiology

The Radiology department offers advanced imaging services, including MRI, CT scan, ultrasound, and X-ray. The team of skilled radiologists provides accurate diagnostics to support effective treatment planning.

Pathology

The Pathology department provides comprehensive diagnostic services through advanced laboratory testing. Services include histopathology, cytology, and clinical pathology, ensuring accurate and timely results for patient care.
