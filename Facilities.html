Facilities



• 7 State-of-the-art Operation Theaters with laminar air flow |42 bedded ICU |19 bedded HDU I Critical Care equipped with the latest technology to deal with critical & post-operative care.

• World class facility for Blood and Marrow Stem Cell Transplantation - The 21 bedded BMT center with BMT Research Lab and Transfusion Services at Dharamshila Hospital, Delhi is India’s first and only world class facility for Blood and Marrow, Stem Cell Transplantation for patients without fully matched family donor. The Centre offers comprehensive evaluation and treatment of blood disorders (Malignant and Non-Malignant). The center is performing Autologous BMT, Allogeneic BMT with fully matched family donor / unrelated donor, cord blood as well as half matched family donor.)

• Linear Accelerators - Triple Energy Linear Accelerators with VMAT technology for IMRT, IGRT, SRS /SRT, SBRT and Respiratory Gated Radiotherapy

• Radiotherapy Treatment Planning Systems - World’s Best Radiotherapy Treatment Planning Systems like Monaco with Monte Carlo Algorithm, ERGO++, CMS Xio and Eclipse.

• Brachytherapy – Microelectron Digital (HDR-V3) Brachytherapy after loader For Precise And Targeted Cancer Treatment. High dose rate Brachytherapy (Microelectron with Ir-192 source) provides intracavitary Brachytherapy for Gynecological Carcinoma cases, intraluminal Brachytherapy for carcinoma esophagus and bronchus cases. Interstitial Brachytherapy for soft tissue sarcomas and head and neck cancers and surface mound treatment is used in specialized situation. Individual patients are planned on Plato Sunrise planning system with CT simulation or X-ray films.

• The Cardiac Centre has dedicated units, which are further rationalized into various sub-specialties of ‘Complete Cardiac Care’. These include: o Clinical and Critical Cardiology
o Non-invasive Cardiology
o Interventional Cardiology
o Electrophysiology and Arrhythmia Services
o Cardiothoracic and Vascular Surgery


• Radio-Imaging department has high end Facilities of PET CT Scan, MRI, CT Scan, Nuclear Scans, Dexa Scan, Mammography etc.

• Department of Renal Sciences has 22 bedded dedicated dialysis unit with most advanced dialysis machines and offers round the clock service with facility for Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT) for Haemon-dynamically unstable patients and Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis. Isolation rooms dedicated to Hepatitis B & C patients are at par with global standards.

• Kidney Transplant – Our Kidney Transplant Unit has comprehensive Kidney Transplant Program including both Autologous and Cadaveric Transplants. The Centre also perform minimally invasive surgery for Renal Donors thereby minimizing post-operative recovery time and hospitalization.

• The Department of Urology is equipped with latest diagnostic and therapeutic facilities like Uroflowmetry, Ultrasound, Doppler, Lithotripsy and Holmium Laser, etc. The Department has dedicated suites for endoscopic procedures ensuring quality treatment as well as complete privacy and comfortable surroundings.
