Services Offered

• Consultation : Out-patient & In-patient

• BMT Research Lab provides Molecular Diagnosis for Leukemia Markers / HLA Typing, Flow Cytometry, Myeloma Profile, Thalassemia Profile, BMT Profiles, CLIN iMacs based cells separation, Immunomagnetic Graft Selection, NK Cell Genotyping, Transplant Cross Matching, Cell Therapy Lab, Cryopreservation of stem cells at -1960C for long term storage etc.

• State-of-the-art Laboratory Services including Cytology, Histopathology, Frozen Sections, Immunohistochemistry, Cytochemistry, Tumor Markers, Hematology, Biochemistry, Clinical Pathology, Microbiology and Serology. The Laboratory Services are accredited by National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL).

• 24X7 Emergency, Trauma and Critical Care Services, Pharmacy Services, Blood Bank, Radiology Services.

• Blood Bank – A state-of-the-art blood bank meeting all standards has been set up with facilities like Aphaeresis, Leukoreduction, Red Cell Serology, blood component separation and stem cell harvesting. In addition, a Blood Irradiator- another First of its Kind facility for specially treated blood for cancer patients.

• Cardiopulmonary Lab has services of ECHO, Stress ECGs, Holter Monitoring, DSE, TMT, Pulmonary Function tests etc.

• Ambulance Services – The Hospital has a fleet of Ambulances fully equipped with Advanced Life Support System for all kinds of emergencies.

• Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Services with Lymphedema Clinic.<

• Boarding and Lodging Services to take care of Boarding and Lodging needs of patients and their attendants during 6 weeks of Radiation (OPD Treatment), Daycare Chemotherapy and Postoperative follow up (When they need not be admitted). We have a guest house within walking distance of the hospital. the guest house is managed by our sister concern and is called Manav Ashray.
